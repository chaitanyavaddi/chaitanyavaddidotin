Refund and cancellation policy

Fair rules for both of us: clear conditions, clear timelines, and a direct line to me if anything goes wrong.

Last updated: July 11, 2026

Overview

I provide custom software services. Because every engagement is custom work, refunds are handled fairly and clearly. This page explains how cancellations and refunds work. Payments for services are collected off site through Razorpay Payment Links or invoices, and all approved refunds go back to the original payment method.

Cancellation before work starts

You receive a full refund of any advance you have paid, minus any payment gateway charges I cannot recover.

Cancellation mid project

You pay for the work completed up to the cancellation date, calculated against the quoted scope. The remainder of your advance is refunded.

Accepted and delivered milestones

Once you have reviewed and accepted a milestone, or a milestone has been delivered, payment for that milestone is not refundable.

Care plans and retainers

Cancel with at least 15 days notice before the next billing cycle. The unused upcoming month is not charged, and you keep the service until the end of the period already paid for.

How to request a refund

  1. Email hellochaitanyavaddi@gmail.com from the address linked to your engagement.
  2. Include your invoice reference and the date of payment.
  3. Describe the reason for the request so I can resolve it quickly.

I acknowledge every refund request within 48 hours and tell you the outcome, or what I need to decide, as quickly as possible.

Processing timeline

  • Approved refunds are initiated within 7 business days of approval.
  • The amount is credited to your original payment method (the same UPI account, card or bank account you paid with).
  • Once initiated, the credit typically reflects within 5 to 10 business days, depending on your bank's or card issuer's processing timelines.
  • If a refund has not reached you after that window, write to me with your reference and I will follow up with the payment gateway.

Disputes

If you are unhappy with an outcome, talk to me first: most issues are resolved in one or two emails. If we still cannot agree, the dispute is handled under the governing law and jurisdiction stated in the terms and conditions: the laws of India, courts at Hyderabad, Telangana.
