Services

Scoped honestly, quoted in writing.

There are no rate cards here, because two projects are never the same. Every engagement begins with a written scope, a timeline and a fixed quote before any payment is made. The price depends on what your project actually requires, nothing more.

01. Web application development

Custom web applications built with Django or FastAPI, from the first database table to the deployed product. Typical projects are internal tools, booking systems, learning platforms, CRMs and customer portals. I build carefully and test what I build, because you will rely on it every day.

  • Authentication, roles and permissions
  • Dashboards, admin panels and reporting
  • Background jobs and scheduled tasks with Celery
  • PostgreSQL schema design and migrations
  • Docker based deployment with CI/CD

Typical timeline: 3 to 6 weeks, depending on scope.

A production ready first version of your SaaS product. I have built multi-tenant platforms for education, publishing and services businesses, and I will tell you plainly what belongs in a first version and what can wait.

  • Subscription billing with Razorpay or Stripe
  • Transactional email, onboarding flows and notifications

  • Third party integrations: WhatsApp, email, CRMs, ERPs

How quoting and payment work

  1. We start by booking a call, so I understand what you need before anything is priced.
  2. You receive a written scope and fixed quote, with a timeline. Nothing is due until you approve it.
  3. Fixed scope projects: 50% advance to begin, 50% on delivery. Longer projects are split into milestones.
  4. Payments are collected securely through Razorpay Payment Links or bank transfer. International clients pay via Stripe, PayPal or wire.
  5. Every payment is preceded by a written quote or invoice that states exactly what is being delivered.
