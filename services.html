How quoting works

From a call to a fixed quote

  1. We start with a free call, so I understand what you need before anything is priced.
  2. You receive a written scope, a timeline and a fixed quote. Nothing is due until you approve it.
  3. Fixed-scope projects run 50% to begin and 50% on delivery, with milestones for longer work.
  4. Payments are collected securely through Razorpay Payment Links or bank transfer. International clients pay via Stripe, PayPal or wire.
  5. Every payment is preceded by a written quote or invoice that states exactly what is being delivered.
