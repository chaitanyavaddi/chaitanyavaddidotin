Privacy policy

I collect very little, I never sell anything about you, and your payment details never touch my systems. Here is the full picture.

Last updated: July 11, 2026

Who I am

This website, chaitanyavaddi.in, is owned and operated by me, Chaitanya Vaddi, a sole proprietor based in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. I provide custom software engineering services. This policy explains what information I collect from you, why I collect it, and how I handle it.

What I collect

  • Contact details. When you write to me by email, phone or WhatsApp, I receive your name, email address, phone number and whatever you choose to share in your message.
  • Project details you share when enquiring. When you engage me for a service, I keep a record of the work scope we agree, the amount paid, the invoice, and the email address the deliverables were sent to.
  • Basic analytics. If analytics are enabled on this site, I see aggregate, non identifying information such as page views and referrers. I do not build individual profiles.
  • Payment data: I never see it. Payments for services are handled by Razorpay, and by Stripe or PayPal for international clients, through secure payment links or invoices. Your card number, UPI ID, net banking credentials and similar payment details go directly to the processor. I never see them and never store them.

How I use your information

  • To deliver the services you commission.
  • To send invoices, receipts and project related communication.
  • To provide setup support, bug fixes and project updates.
  • To meet legal and tax obligations under Indian law.

What I never do

  • I never sell, rent or trade your personal information to anyone.
  • I never send marketing spam. You hear from me about your own projects, nothing else.
  • I never share your information with third parties, except the payment and email processors listed below, and only to the extent needed to serve you.

Cookies and local storage

This site stores exactly one thing in your browser: your light or dark theme preference, saved in localStorage. There are no tracking cookies, no advertising pixels and no fingerprinting.

Third party processors

I rely on a small number of established providers to run my business:

  • Razorpay for payments in India (UPI, cards, net banking) via secure payment links or invoices.
  • Stripe and PayPal for international payments where applicable.
  • My email provider, which carries the messages and deliverables I send.

Each of these processors handles your data under its own privacy policy, and each is responsible for securing the payment information you enter on its pages.

Data retention

I keep business records, such as invoices and project details, for as long as Indian tax and accounting law requires. Everything else is deleted when it is no longer needed, or earlier if you ask me to remove it.

Your rights

You can ask me at any time to:

  • Show you the personal information I hold about you.
  • Correct anything that is inaccurate.
  • Delete your information, subject to the records I am legally required to keep.

Email me at hellochaitanyavaddi@gmail.com and I will respond promptly.

Children

This website and my services are not directed at children under 18. I do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has shared personal information with me, contact me and I will delete it.

Changes to this policy

If I change this policy, I will update this page and revise the date at the top. Continued use of the site after a change means you accept the updated policy.
